About the Saturday Morning Run

The Saturday morning run has been held for more than thirty years. We meet every Saturday at 9 o'clock at the Mountain Home Inn parking lot. Details are on the Tamalpa Runners website  here.

This Saturday Morning Run website currently has just a small collection of photos from a few recent runs. If you have or know of relevant pictures, stories, or other material, especially from more than a few years ago, please contact the website administrator.
