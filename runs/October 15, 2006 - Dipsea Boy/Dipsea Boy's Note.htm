Note from Dipsea Boy

 

Thanks for taking up the challenge of the 2006 Dipsea Boy Invitational Cross County Race.  This race was all about the course, as Jamie Berns had the second-fastest time both up the mountain and down (topping such tough, but

route-challenged, competitors as Greg Nacco, Jed Tukman and Rob Flatland). The best course without question was the one run by Roy and Jamie.

 

Please check the math on your own splits to see if they add up (and send me any revisions).  The times that Dave Pinto called at the top were 1 minute and 2 seconds less than the actual times.

 

Please feel very free to send me stories or anecdotes about the race, as I will probably concoct a story for the Gazette or the Saturday Morning Run website.
